### Procedural Requirements

The following procedural steps are generally applicable for implementing community service programs for first-time offenders, though practitioners should verify jurisdiction-specific requirements:

2. Selection of appropriate community service activities that align with the offender's skills and interests.
3. Establishment of clear goals and expectations for the community service program.
